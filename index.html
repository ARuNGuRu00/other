1.What is the problem?

   We had a Bluetooth speaker due to some miss handling its RGB light button was brocken. We recured the button While accessing the speakers PCB, which brocked connecting points and mounting points. A layer of the PCB at the mounting endpoints were completly cameout with the button, these end surfaces are more important because when we apply focus to the button these mounting surfaces will transfers the pressure to the PCB so that the button can be stable for next press.

2.What we did?

   After analysing the PCB, we are sure that the connecting ends of the button can be soldered as it is, but the mounting end are more challenging because I desided to solder the mounting end with the near by buttons mounting ends but they had a bit of gap, so it may are mayn't be soldered properly.

   We prepared to solder after a while, first we soldered the connecting ends properly with out any miss lead, Then we tried to solder a the moulding ends with near by ends as a predicted where a bit of gap and the soldering tip also made the process hard. So I melted a bit more soldering wire and applyed between the button like in a building construction :) which made the mounting ends to make a connect for support, it worked!!!.
